What is ABA?

Applied behavior analysis (ABA) treatment an evidence-based best practice for the safe and effective treatment of autism and is recommended by the U.S. Surgeon General.

Often referred to as ABA or ABA therapy , the treatment is based on the science of learning and behaviors with a focus on antecedents and consequences. The goal of ABA is to decrease problem behaviors and increase positive behaviors.

We use a variety of techniques in ABA treatment to teach important skills to your child in a way he or she learns best.

From blowing out a birthday candle to making eye contact and saying hello, we chart, measure, and celebrate every milestone.

We know accountability is critical to success.
