Why Great Heights

Great Heights is dedicated to success. We provide personalized applied behavioral analysis (ABA) treatment for children with autism. We take time to learn the individualized needs and unique challenges of every child, and deliver personalized, proven treatment. We are your partner on this journey, committed to building a brighter future for your child and family.

Dedication


Care, respect, teamwork, continuous learning and appreciation are the foundational values that we instill in everything we do at Great Heights. These principles guide our commitment to the success of each and every child. There is always hope.



Progress


Progress is the result of a proven approach. Applied behavior analysis is a scientific discipline that focuses on the application of learning principles to change behavior.



Compassion


We know how challenging and overwhelming an autism diagnosis can be for families. Great Heights is a place of hope. We commit to working with you to build a brighter future for your child and your family.




What is ABA?

ABA Treatment for Children with Autism


  • Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) treatment is based on the science of learning and behaviors. ABA is considered an evidence-based ͞best͟ practice for the safe and effective treatment of autism, endorsed by the U.S. Surgeon General. ABA therapy is structured, measures progress through data collection, and provides positive strategies for changing responses and behaviors.
  • We use a variety of techniques in ABA treatment to teach important skills to your child in a way he or she learns best. From blowing out a birthday candle to making eye contact and saying hello, we chart, measure, and celebrate every milestone. We know accountability is critical to success.
